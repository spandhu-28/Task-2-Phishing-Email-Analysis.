Phishing Email Analysis

This task involves analyzing a suspicious email to identify phishing characteristics. The email contains multiple indicators such as a spoofed sender address, urgent language, and a misleading link. The sender’s domain is slightly altered, indicating email spoofing. The message creates urgency and fear to trick the user into clicking a malicious link. The URL provided does not match the official website, which is a major sign of phishing. These characteristics confirm that the email is a phishing attempt designed to steal user information.
